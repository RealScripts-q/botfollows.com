How to Find Your Cookie:

  1. Open the website in your browser.
  2. Right-click anywhere on the page and select Inspect.
  3. Navigate to the Application tab.
  4. Under Storage in the left sidebar, select Cookies.
  5. Click on the website URL in the list of cookies.
  6. Look for the cookie named _|WARNING:-DO-NOT-SHARE-THIS.
  7. Copy its value and paste it below.
