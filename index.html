How to Find Your Roblox Cookie:

  1. Go to www.roblox.com in your browser.
  2. Right-click anywhere on the page and select Inspect.
  3. Navigate to the Application tab in the Developer Tools.
  4. Under the Storage section on the left sidebar, click on Cookies.
  5. Select www.roblox.com from the list of domains.
  6. Look for a cookie named _ROBLOSECURITY or any similar cookie that may contain your session information.
